Pricing Factors

What Affects Your Rate

Distance

Mileage is the primary factor. Longer distances typically have lower per-mile rates than short urgent runs.

Equipment

Vehicle size affects cost. Cargo vans are most economical; 26' box trucks cost more but carry larger loads.

Urgency

Same-day and critical shipments command premium rates. Scheduled pickups with flexibility cost less.

Time of Day

After-hours and weekend pickups may have surcharges. Standard business hours offer best rates.

Special Handling

Liftgate, team drivers, hazmat certification, and TWIC requirements add to the base rate.

Market Conditions

Carrier availability in your area affects pricing. High-demand lanes may cost more during peak times.
